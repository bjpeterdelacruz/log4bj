I Passed Cloud+ CV0-002!

February 8, 2018

I took the beta version of the CompTIA Cloud+ CV0-002 certification exam in August 2017. This morning, I received an e-mail from Pearson VUE that the exam results were available on CompTIA's website, so I logged in to my account and found out that I passed!

I will be taking the beta version of the CompTIA PenTest+ PT1-001 certification exam on April 2, 2018, one week after I take the (ISC)2 CSSLP exam on March 26. I will brush up on Bash, Python, and Ruby, and I will read bits and pieces of the CEH Certified Ethical Hacker All-in-One Exam Guide to prepare for the PenTest+ exam.
